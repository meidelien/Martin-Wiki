Sustainable Development Goals

Best Sustainable Development Special Prize

We encourage you to demonstrate how you have evaluated your project ideas against one or more of the SDGs, how you’ve consulted with SDG stakeholders, and how you’ve begun to form collaborations with other iGEM teams around the SDGs. You’re encouraged to look back at previous iGEM projects to evaluate them against the SDGs and build upon them.

To compete for the Best Sustainable Development prize, please describe your work on this page and also fill out the description on the judging form.
