Background

At the University of Oslo, participation in iGEM is sponsored by the university as a two-semester course (course code: MBV4910IGEM) and is evaluated on a pass or fail basis. There are no supporting course materials, for this course as the course consists of a student-driven interdisciplinary project in synthetic biology. The course starts in March and finishes after the Giant Jamboree.
